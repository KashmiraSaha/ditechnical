ABOUT US

The DI TECHNICAL PVT LTD run by the Department of Services & Sports (Services wing) in collaboration with different private organizations play an important role in providing low cost computer training to the student across the state enabling them to prepare themselves fit for employment. About 1,000 (One Thousand) DI TECHNICAL PVT LTD are now running across the state. Every year thousands of student are getting themselves eligible to survive in the competitive job market successfully being trained from these training centres in various courses.
